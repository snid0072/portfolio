About Me

Hello! My name is Evan Snider, and I’m currently an IMD student at Algonquin College, where I’m exploring the world of design and development. I have a passion for the simpler things in life—whether it’s enjoying a quiet afternoon, taking a walk in nature, or discovering new hobbies. I believe in cherishing the small, beautiful moments that make life meaningful, and I try to bring that sense of simplicity into my work. I'm always seeking new adventures, whether through learning, exploring unfamiliar places, or meeting new people, and these experiences fuel my creativity. My work reflects my passions, combining technology, design, and creativity to create functional and visually appealing projects. If you enjoy what I create, I hope we can collaborate in the future. I believe that great things happen when like-minded people come together, and I look forward to the possibility of working together to create something amazing!
